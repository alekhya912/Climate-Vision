Low Emission Scenario

Assumes rapid global transition to renewable energy, substantial reduction in carbon emissions, and widespread adoption of carbon capture technologies. Global temperature increase limited to approximately 1.5°C by 2100.

Medium Emission Scenario

Assumes moderate climate policies with gradual transition to cleaner energy sources. Some reduction in emissions growth but continued reliance on fossil fuels. Global temperature increase of approximately 2.5-3.0°C by 2100.

High Emission Scenario

Assumes business-as-usual with minimal climate policy intervention and continued heavy reliance on fossil fuels. Global temperature increase exceeding 4.0°C by 2100, with significant environmental consequences.
